How it works?

VIP

Too lazy to shop groceries after work? Forgot some party stuff but don't have time to buy? Why not try V3? Anyone can sign up as a VIP and easily order from local shops, our algorithm will optimize the delivery for you. We are designed to ship products in a quick and cost-saving manner.

Vendor

If you are a store owner worried that you'll fade out in the e-commerce age, this is your chance! Welcome to sign up as our vendor. V3 will not only provide you with an online presence, but also bring more sales. Simply synchronize your inventory to our platform, and many more consumers can view and order. Going online will bring you more traffic and thus more revenue.

Valet

Want to make some money on your way to work? Want to work flexibly without routine? Join our Valet team! As a valet you will pick-up orders and delivery to consumers. The cool thing is, you can totally be your own boss and set your own schedule, we got your back.
